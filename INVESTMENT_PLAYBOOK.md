## UNIVERSAL RULES

These apply to every position, no exceptions.

### Position Limits
- **Max single position:** 15% of portfolio
- **Max tier allocation:** Tier 1: 60%, Tier 2: 30%, Tier 3: 20%
- **Max correlated cluster:** 30% (stocks with correlation > 0.7)
- **Cash reserve:** Always hold 5-10% cash for dip-buying opportunities

### Rebalancing
- **Trigger:** Any position drifts > 5% from target allocation
- **Frequency:** Review monthly, rebalance quarterly at minimum
- **Method:** Trim overweight positions, add to underweight. Never sell a winner just because it grew — only trim if allocation rules are breached

### Review Schedule
- **Weekly:** Check stop-loss levels and catalyst calendar
- **Monthly:** Review bear scenario probabilities — has anything changed?
- **Quarterly:** Full portfolio review against all exit criteria below
- **Immediately after:** Any bear scenario event (efficiency breakthrough, regulatory ruling, rate decision, earnings miss)

## WHAT "THESIS INVALIDATION" ACTUALLY LOOKS LIKE

The core thesis is: *AI datacenter buildout drives energy demand, making energy infrastructure stocks undervalued.*

**The thesis is INTACT if:**
- Hyperscaler capex continues growing (check quarterly earnings from MSFT, META, AMZN, GOOGL)
- New datacenter announcements continue
- Power purchase agreements continue being signed
- Uranium supply deficit persists
- Natural gas demand for power gen grows

**The thesis is WEAKENING if:**
- Hyperscaler capex guidance is cut 2 consecutive quarters
- AI efficiency gains reduce compute-per-watt faster than demand grows (Jevons Paradox fails)
- 3+ major PPA renegotiations occur
- New reactor construction timelines slip by 2+ years industry-wide

**The thesis is BROKEN if:**
- Hyperscalers pivot to on-site generation (cutting out utilities entirely)
- AI winter — corporate AI budgets slashed broadly
- Nuclear regulatory environment becomes hostile (NRC blocks all SMRs + FERC reverses co-location permanently)
- All three happen simultaneously

**If thesis breaks:** Liquidate Tier 3 immediately. Reduce Tier 2 by 50%. Hold Tier 1 names with contracted cash flows (KMI, LNG) — these survive even without the AI thesis.
